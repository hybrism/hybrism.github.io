ABOUT ME

Hi!I always find it quite awkward to write these kinds of presentations, as I'm sure most people do. I always find it easier to write lists, so here's a very nostalgic and game-centered bullet list about my life:

  • I was born in 1990, I have three sisters and one brother and I can say with confidence that I'm the biggest nerd of the bunch.

  • Back in the 90s we had a family shared Macintosh computer, one of the first games I remember playing (together with my dad because my English wasn't up to par then) is Indiana Jones and the Fate of Atlantis. We had to play with a walkthrough because we kept getting deadlocked.

  • I was not only a regular nerd I was also a huge horse nerd. Another game I played spent a lot of time with as a kid was a horse customization game called Paint n Play Pony. Customization is a big thing in my life, and if a game I play contains any kind of customization it automatically receives a stamp of approval from me.

  • Related to the point above, in my early teens my dream was to become a character designer. I was always drawing new characters with cool outfits and weapons and coming up with background stories for them, highly influenced by the Final Fantasy-series and Kingdom Hearts. I don't draw anymore, but I still come up with little background stories to every character I come across that doesn't already have one.

  • I started working in a clothing store when I was 18, not really pursuing the character designer dream but kind of in the same fashion (pun intended?) There I went from working the shop floor, was promoted to department manager then floor manager and lastly promoted to store controller (basically assistant store manager). There I discovered my penchant for optimizing the store's work routines. Placing the right person or item in the right time and place or making sure that everything happens in the correct order became like a game for me.

  • I was also the one in charge of handling the store's stock routines, and some time around 2018 I was chosen to become a superuser for a new stock management system. Working in close contact with the assigned IT-department made me realize that this was actually something one can work with, creating systems for more efficient workflows. Ultimately this realization along with some other things led me to go looking for a new career.

  • In 2022 I sent my application to The Game Assembly, got accepted into the scool, 2.5 years later and here I am! A fully educated Game Programmer, just starting my journey into the games industry.

TL;DR: I'm a huge nerd that loves character customization and I have a penchant for creating systems that make peoples lives easier. Oh, and I'm a newly graduated Game Programmer.If you'd wish to get in touch with me, please feel free to send me an Email or write to me on LinkedIn, I’d love to hear from you :)
